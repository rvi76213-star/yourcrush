ADMIN COMMAND: !stop

Description: Stop bot or current operations

Usage: !stop [feature] [reason]

Parameters:
  feature - What to stop (bot, command, auto, all)
  reason - Optional reason

Examples:
  !stop
  !stop bot
  !stop command "murgi"
  !stop all "Maintenance"

Notes:
  - Without parameter: stops current command
  - "bot": stops entire bot
  - "command [name]": stops specific command
  - "auto": stops auto-features
  - "all": stops everything
